About Me

Hi there, My name is Yana, and it is very nice meeting you!

I am proud Ukrainian. I have a Bachelor’s degree in International Business. It was my parents choice - at age 17 I did not have a word to say. Right after completing my degree, I moved to the USA. My most recent work experience was an office administrator in a construction equipment rental company and as well as for a roofing company. At some point I started to see that I could not progress and I wanted to change my career path. A few of my friends advised me to look into the IT field because they really enjoyed their jobs and they ALL were advancing in their companies quickly.

As of today, I am 100% committed to building my Software Developing career. I would not consider any other opportunity now. I want to be someone who makes the difference in the field.
